Our Initiatives

Education & IT Training

We run the Unique Skills Academy of IT Education, affiliated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University. Offering PGDCA, DCA, and job-oriented courses to empower youth with industry-relevant digital skills.

Visit Academy Website

Women & Child Welfare

Uplifting women and children through self-defense classes, nutrition sessions, and essential distribution in Anganwadis. We focus on early childhood education and creating a secure, informed society.

Health & Hygiene Awareness

Regular health camps, blood donation drives, and hygiene awareness campaigns. We provide free medical check-ups and immunization support to make healthcare accessible for the underprivileged.

Environment & Cleanliness

Promoting sustainability through the Green India Mission. We organize tree plantation drives and Swachhta Abhiyan campaigns to instill a culture of cleanliness and civic responsibility.

Community Support & Relief

Standing by the community during festivals and emergencies. We distribute food, clothes, and hygiene essentials to the most vulnerable sections of society with dignity and compassion.

Youth Development & Culture

Fostering the growth of future leaders through skill development workshops, public speaking, sports meets, and cultural engagement activities that nurture creativity and confidence.

Handicraft Mela & Entrepreneurship

Supporting artisans and small-scale entrepreneurs by organizing fairs that showcase handmade products. A platform to boost income generation and celebrate cultural heritage.

Women Entrepreneur Awards

Celebrating International Women's Day by honoring outstanding women entrepreneurs. This initiative recognizes courage, innovation, and resilience in business to inspire others.

Mitti Ke Ganesh Workshop

An eco-friendly initiative inviting the community to create clay Ganesh idols. Spreading awareness about pollution-free celebrations and using natural materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Lalitamba Social Welfare Society registered?

Yes, we are a registered society with the Registration Number 01/01/01/24673/12. We are also 12A and 80G certified.

Are donations tax-exempt?

Yes, all donations made to our society are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! You can join us for various drives like teaching, tree plantation, or food distribution. Fill out the contact form below with the purpose "Volunteering".

What courses are offered at Unique Skills Academy?

We offer PGDCA, DCA, and other job-oriented IT courses affiliated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University.

How can I invite the society for a health/awareness camp?

Please contact us at 0755-4254278 or email lalitamba.society@gmail.com with your request details.
